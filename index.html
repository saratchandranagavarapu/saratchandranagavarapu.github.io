Sarat Chandra Nagavarapu

I am a Senior Research Scientist with the Advanced Remanufacturing and Technology Centre (ARTC), Agency for Science, Technology And Research (A*STAR), Singapore. I received my Ph.D. in Systems and Control Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Bombay in 2016, advised by Prof. Leena Vachhani and Prof. Arpita Sinha. I received my M.Tech. in Information Technology from International Institute of Information Technology (IIIT) Bangalore in 2010, and my M.Sc. and B.Sc. in Electronics from Andhra University, Visakhapatnam in 2006 and 2008 respectively. Before joining ARTC, I was with the Institute for Infocomm Research (I2R) , A*STAR, and held postdoctoral research positions at the Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, working with Prof. Amal Chandran (Former Director, Satellite Research Centre (SaRC), NTU), Prof. Daniel E. Hastings (MIT), and Prof. Justin Dauwels across SaRC, CETRAN (ERI@N), and the ST Engineering - NTU Robotics Corporate Laboratory.

My research interests include multi-robot coordination, multi-agent path planning, autonomous navigation, optimization, AI/ML, intelligent transportation systems, and space robotics.

Research interests

  • AI and optimization techniques for intelligent transportation applications
  • Routing and optimization using meta-heuristics for large scale fleet management
  • Strategies for autonomous vehicle (robot) navigation with collision avoidance
  • Multi-robot exploration & mapping with decentralized coordination
  • Classical and lifelong Multi-Agent Path Finding (MAPF) for multi-robot coordination
  • Space debris removal from low Earth orbits using CubeSats with robotic arms
  • Air traffic management and optimizing airport ground & runway operations
